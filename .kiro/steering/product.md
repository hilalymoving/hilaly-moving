# Product Overview

**Hilaly Moving** is a high-conversion, bilingual landing page for a furniture moving and logistics service in Egypt.

## Core Purpose
Lead generation via WhatsApp instead of traditional backend forms. Users fill out a booking form that generates a formatted WhatsApp message sent directly to the business owner.

## Key Features
- **WhatsApp-First**: Custom message builder for frictionless booking
- **Bilingual**: Arabic (RTL) primary, English (LTR) support
- **Theme System**: Light/Dark mode with CSS variables
- **CMS Architecture**: Centralized content management in `cms.js`
- **Admin Panel**: Client-side content editor (password-protected)
- **Scroll Animations**: Custom `Reveal` component for entrance effects
- **Promotional Popup**: Timer-based offer popup with configurable countdown
- **Firebase Integration**: Firestore for content persistence across sessions

## Target Market
Egyptian families and businesses needing furniture moving services, primarily Arabic-speaking users.

## Business Model
Service-based lead generation. The site converts visitors into WhatsApp conversations for quote requests.
